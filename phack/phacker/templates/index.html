p-hacker

The p-test is widely regarded as a bastion of scientific achievement, and everyone knows that getting the p-value you want means that you conducted groundbreaking and accurate research. But, maybe that publication is due in a few days, and you find that your data isn’t really resulting in a low p-value, so that’s why we’re launching p-hacker: a tool that helps you get the results you want, faster.

If you’re affiliated with graduate school admissions, please reach out and consider letting us in. We think p-hacker has the potential to significantly improve your institution’s research throughput.
